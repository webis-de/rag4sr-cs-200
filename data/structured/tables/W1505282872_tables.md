| RQ | Research Question |
|---|---|
| RQ1 | What are the initiatives and experiences of SPC in Software Engineering, the SPC control charts being used, statistical techniques, and tools that complement its application? |
| RQ2 | What are the benefits and limitations of SPC identified in the context of software, and the evidence of the alleged benefits? |
| RQ3 | Can SPC be applied to companies with lower levels of maturity or emerging companies? And what would be the conditions, settings, features, and environments to apply SPC in software processes? |
| RQ4 | What are the methodologies that support proper implementation of SPC in quantitative management? |
| RQ5 | What activities and control methods are being proposed for monitoring software distributed processes in companies with lower levels of maturity or emerging ones? |
